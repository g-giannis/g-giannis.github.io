<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
<em>Last updated: December 2025</em>
  &nbsp
  
<p>Thank you for using <strong>Noah</strong>. Your privacy matters greatly, and Noah is designed from the ground up to protect it.</p>

<h2>1. Overview</h2>
<p>Noah is a simple, offline-first baby tracker. Your data stays on your device and is never sent to third parties.</p>

<h2>2. What Data Noah Stores</h2>
<p>Noah stores only the information you manually enter, such as feedings and sleep.</p>
<p>Everything is saved locally using secure Apple system storage.</p>

<h2>3. Backups</h2>
<p>You may export a backup manually. Backups only go where you choose (e.g., the Files app or iCloud Drive).</p>

<h2>4. No Cloud Sync or Accounts</h2>
<p>Noah does not provide accounts, cloud syncing, or server-based storage. All data remains on your device.</p>

<h2>5. Crash Reports</h2>
<p>If enabled in iOS settings, Apple may share anonymous crash logs. These contain no personal data and are controlled by Apple.</p>

<h2>6. Third-Party Services</h2>
<p>Noah uses no analytics, advertising systems, trackers, or external SDKs.</p>

<h2>7. Contact</h2>
<p>If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact me at:</p>
<p><strong>contact@ggiannis.com</strong></p>
